Symmetry

What is it?

Symmetrical balance is a powerful way to organize content as well as to create a focus in a web page. Symmetrical features could be split down the middle and the two sides would be identical. Symmetry creates balance which can make the audience feel comfortable and at ease. When a website does not have symmetrical features it can cause uneasiness and chaos within the page. As a result, the viewer may acquire a feeling of unrest when visiting the website.

Why I chose it?

I chose Questionable Characters to showcase the effective use of symmetry. This creatively used symmetry to provide feedback from two individuals when asked a question. Everything on this page is symmetrical with the placement and design of the logo, centered headline text, and the two columns featuring the writer's responses. This site is very organized and the content is provided effectively.
