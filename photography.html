Photography

What is it?

Photography can have a very powerful impact on a design. It gives the audience a visual element as well as context for the provided information. Photography has to be relevant to the subject matter in order to compliment the other elements on the page. The look of the image makes a difference too. It has to blend in the with the design and become a part of it rather than arbitrarily placed on a page. A photograph can create confusion on a page if used incorrectly.

Why I chose it?

I chose Blind Barber to showcase the use of powerful photography on a website. The entire top of the fold for each page is a photograph. I feel that this makes such a statement for this website. It gives a powerful image to focus on and leads me to scroll down in curiosity of what lies below the image. It gets me to navigate through the site versus just staying at the top. Each image is relevant to the content specific to each page as well.
