Alignment

What is it?

Alignment is the concept of lining up elements on a page to provide the content in an organized manner. If done right, it can help to direct the viewer's eye through the page or to where the designer wants it to go. Order and direction can be accomplished through alignment. It works in conjunction with other design elements such as white space and the grid in order to produce the best results.

Why I chose it?

I chose Jason Santa Maria's website to showcase the effective use of alignment. There are many elements of text and images incorporated on this page. They are presented very orderly with the use of alignment with the two columns. The spacing really separates the elements which eliminates confusion and chaos. The page flows nicely and looks very organized.
