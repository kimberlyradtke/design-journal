Emphasis

What is it?

Emphasis is the idea that a particular element should stand out or take precedence over other elements on the web page. This creates a focal point that the designer wants the audience to take notice of first. Emphasis can be achieved in a variety of ways. This may include use of different typography styles and sizes, contrasting color, scale, etc.

Why I chose it?

I chose Urbanoff to showcase the effective use of emphasis because of its bold use of typography to instantly grab the users attention. This site is a portfolio website for an individual. The most important thing to take away from this site is the artists name. It is his brand, so it should be highlighted first. This site is very effective in doing that by using very large and visually appealing font to create his logo. The horizontal lines frame the name making it stand out more.
